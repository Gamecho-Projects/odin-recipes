Roasted vegetable salad with spicy tahini dressing

Photo by Shameel Mukkath

Fresh, colorful, and full of texture, this mixed vegetable tahini salad is a perfect way to turn simple ingredients into something vibrant and satisfying. Crisp vegetables provide plenty of crunch, while the creamy tahini dressing ties everything together with nutty depth and a hint of tang. It’s hearty enough to stand on its own as a light meal, but also makes a refreshing side that pairs well with grilled meats, grains, or warm flatbreads.

The beauty of this salad is its versatility—you can use whatever vegetables you have on hand and let the dressing do the heavy lifting. The tahini adds richness, lemon juice brightens it up, and a touch of garlic gives it that extra kick. Once tossed together, every bite balances freshness with creaminess, making it one of those dishes you’ll keep coming back to.

Tahini dressing ingredients

  • 4 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, finely grated or minced
  • 1 tsp maple syrup or honey (optional)
  • 3-5 tbsp cold water (depending on thickness)
  • Salt to taste
  • Pinch of ground cumin

Instructions

  1. Chop all vegetables of your choosing to desired size.
  2. Mix all ingredients together apart from the water and salt
  3. Slowly poor cold water one tablespoon at a time until you have achieved your desired consistency.
  4. Taste and adjust—add more lemon for brightness, tahini for creaminess, or water for a lighter texture.
  5. Drizzle over your salad and toss well to coat.
